  • The Spider/Robot category includes accesses by search engine "spiders" and other automated programs which roam the Web. Offline Browsers are similar to the search engine robots, but are sent by specific individuals interested in viewing content off-line. Download Managers include FTP clients and "download accelerators." Link Checkers are, self-evidently, automated link monitoring and "uptime monitoring" tools. The E-Mail Harvester category reflects accesses by robots specifically searching for e-mail addresses, usually for the purpose of adding them to "spam" mailing lists. Finally, the Other Agent category, of course, includes everything which can't, for whatever reason, be identified as belonging to one of the other categories.
